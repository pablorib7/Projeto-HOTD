Balerion

Balerion, called the Black Dread, was a dragon of House Targaryen. He was ridden by King Aegon I Targaryen during the War of Conquest.

Vermithor

Vermithor, also known as the Bronze Fury, was ridden by King Jaehaerys I Targaryen. Vermithor was one of the largest dragons, second only to Vhagar during the Dance of the Dragons.

Arrax

Arrax was a dragon ridden by Prince Lucerys Velaryon during the Dance of the Dragons.

Caraxes

Caraxes, also called the Blood Wyrm, was the dragon ridden by Prince Aemon Targaryen during the reign of King Jaehaerys I Targaryen and later by Prince Daemon Targaryen.

Seasmoke

Seasmoke was a light gray dragon. He was large enough for combat during the Dance, but still a young dragon, and more agile in the air than his older siblings.

Vermax

Vermax was ridden by Prince Jacaerys Velaryon. He thrived and grew every year. Vermax became ill-tempered when near snow, ice, and cold. His color is not described in the books.

Vhagar

Vhagar was a female dragon ridden by Visenya during the Conquest, alongside Aegon the Conqueror's Balerion and her sister Rhaenys's Meraxes.
